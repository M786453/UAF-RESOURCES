### PSYCHOLOGICAL CONTRACT ####

- system of beliefs
- encompasses:
	* what actions expected of the employee
	* what response they expect in return from employer

- positive pyscho. contract:
	* both employee and employer agree on mutual expectations

- performance management play important part in this case

########## PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT VS PERFORMANCE APPRAISAL ###########

##### PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT #######

- Joint process through dialogue
- continous review
- rating less common
- Flexible process
- minimum documentation
- less likely to direct link with pay
- owned by line managers
